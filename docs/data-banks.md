# Good Data Bank vs Bad Data Bank

## Good Data Bank
Contains fully validated, complete, structured financial packages.

Used for:
- Future SLM training and evaluation
- Reliable credit memo generation
- Benchmarking across clients
- Fast reprocessing and reuse

## Bad Data Bank
Contains incomplete, mislabeled, inconsistent, or corrupted documents.

Used for:
- Remediation workflows
- Identifying recurring upload issues
- Tracking problematic vendors/submitters
- Compliance review of rejected inputs

This separation supports governance, quality tracking, and long-term SLM readiness.
